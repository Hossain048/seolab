Who I Am

I'm Md Abu Hossain Mohalder, a 4th year (1st Semester) Computer Science and Engineering student at the University of Barishal, Bangladesh (Roll: 21CSE048, Session: 2020–21). I'm passionate about machine learning, algorithms, and building things for the web.

What I'm Working On

My B.Sc. thesis investigates AI-generated phishing email detection — specifically measuring the "detection gap" when traditional ML models trained on human-written phishing are tested against LLM-generated attacks. This research extends existing work in the IEEE ICMI 2024 proceedings.

I'm also completing coursework in Information Retrieval and SEO (CSE-4108), which led to this blog as a hands-on SEO project.
